## 📚 Academic Session: Hands-On Machine Learning
### Chapter 2: End-to-End Machine Learning Project
Presenter: Ermia Moghadami

We analyzed the entire lifecycle of an ML project. The discussion followed the 8-step blueprint from the book:
1.  Look at the Big Picture: Defining the business objective and selecting a performance measure (RMSE vs. MAE).
2.  Get the Data: Automation of data fetching and creating workspace environments.
3.  Explore & Visualize: Identifying patterns, correlations (Pearson’s r), and handling geographical data.
4.  Prepare the Data: Essential preprocessing including handling missing values, encoding categorical text, and feature scaling (Min-Max vs. Standardization).
5.  Select & Train a Model: Testing baseline models like Linear Regression and Decision Trees.
6.  Fine-Tune the Model: Utilizing Grid Search and Randomized Search for hyperparameter optimization.
7.  Present the Solution: Documenting findings and highlighting what was learned.
8.  Launch, Monitor, & Maintain: The "MLOps" aspect—deploying the model and checking for system performance rot.
